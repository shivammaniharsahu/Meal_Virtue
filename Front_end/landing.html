We aim towards minimizing food wastage, which is a source of life for everyone

When throwing food away, try to donate first, if you can't, then compost it, and if you can't do that, throw it in the trash. Keeping this order in mind minimizes the amount of food that ends up on a landfill.

  • Less food wastage is financially helpful
  • Give it to us, we will redistribute it
  • Food recycling helps in making a lot of stuff like manure etc. Don;t throw it in the trash

Donating to the needy is a great way to improve the conditions in your neighborhood or community. Donating food to the worthy people or organizations helps counter poverty, hunger and at the same time, it can improve harmony, friendliness, and trust among residents.
